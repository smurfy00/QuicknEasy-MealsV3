Garlic Prawns

Ingredients;

  • 500gms Raw prawns
  • 3 tbs of Minced Garlic
  • 300ml Cooking Cream
  • Salt & Pepper for taste
  • ½ cup of white wine
  • ¼ cup shopped shallots
  • 2 tbs of butter
  • Dried chopped parsley flakes (garnish)
  • Boiled or Steam Rice enough for 2 to 4 people.
  • Crust Bread Loaf

Method; Cut the backs of the prawns, not all the way through, and devein. Melt butter in pan with minced garlic. Add prawns and keep stirring until you see prawns change color. Add cream and wine. Bring to boil and then simmer. Add shallots and season with salt and pepper. Let simmer for about 10 mins until the sauce has reduced. Take pan of heat. Serve with boiled or steamed rice into bowls. Put garlic prawns on top. Put a generous amount of sauce on top. Sprinkle with dried chopped parsley flakes. Serve with nice crusty bread.

Eat and Enjoy
